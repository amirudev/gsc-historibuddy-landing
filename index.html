What is HistoriBuddy?

Your AI-powered companion for historical exploration

HistoriBuddy is a mobile app that helps tourists and history enthusiasts discover and learn about historical objects. Using your phone's camera, you can take photos of historical artifacts, buildings, or monuments, and get detailed information through an AI-powered chat interface.
